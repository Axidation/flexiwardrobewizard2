What kind of space are you working with?

Straight Wall

The wardrobe will be installed on a straight wall and will be enclosed by an end panel either side.

Enclosed Walls

The wardrobe will be installed between two existing walls and requires no end panels.

Left Corner

The wardrobe will be installed to a left corner wall and requires an end panel on the right hand side.

Right Corner

The wardrobe will be installed to a right corner wall and requires an end panel on the left hand side.
